1 Q3 Target Overview

Three focused targets to drive consistent content output and better campaign connection.

📝

Industrial Tutorial / Article Production

Goal

Produce at least 2–3 industrial tutorials or articles per week.

Measurement
  • Minimum: 2 content pieces per week
  • Ideal: 3 content pieces per week
  • Monthly target: 8–12 content pieces
  • Q3 target: 24–36 content pieces
Purpose: Support product visibility, technical education, and marketing campaign readiness.
🎬

Solehin Video Integration

Goal

Include suitable Solehin TikTok video content in Cytron YouTube and connect the video back into related articles.

Measurement
  • Identify suitable videos weekly
  • Repurpose selected videos for YouTube
  • Include relevant videos inside tutorial / article pages
  • Track articles with video support
Purpose: Make industrial articles more engaging by combining written content with short-form video explanation.
📧

ActiveCampaign Coordination with Iffah

Goal

Make sure targeted tutorials are submitted and tracked for ActiveCampaign usage.

Measurement
  • Identify targeted tutorials for ActiveCampaign
  • Prepare supporting content / copy where needed
  • Coordinate with Iffah for campaign inclusion
  • Track which tutorials are added into ActiveCampaign
  • Monitor clicks, engagement, or sales tracking where possible
Purpose: Ensure tutorial content is not only published, but also used in follow-up campaigns to support audience engagement and product interest.
