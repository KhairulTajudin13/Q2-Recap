Why This Objective Was Needed

Team feedback showed that Cytron's tutorial content had strong value, but the discovery experience was still fragmented. Users from different audiences - education, industry, commerce, partnership, and customer support - needed clearer tutorial pathways, better categorization, easier navigation, and stronger product/resource hubs.

This objective helped address that gap by turning existing tutorials into a more organized and promotion-ready content pool, so selected tutorials could support product visibility, user learning, and marketing channel engagement.

Appraisal Summary

For Objective 1, I focused on increasing the visibility of selected tutorials by gathering and organizing 86 tutorials across key product categories such as Motor Driver, Robotics, RDK, IRIV PiControl, Milesight, Raspberry Pi, NVIDIA, EDU PICO V2, and CYBORC.

During Q2, 12 tutorials with complete tracking data were promoted through suitable channels including Telegram communities, Cytron X, and ActiveCampaign-related planning. Their total views increased from 25,822 to 30,978 by 8 July, showing an increase of 5,156 views or 20.0%.

The strongest promotion period was May, where 8 tutorials contributed 4,423 additional views. Robotics-related tutorials promoted through Telegram groups performed especially well, contributing 2,609 additional views, while the RDK X5 Quick Start Guide promoted through the CytronRDK Telegram group gained 1,173 additional views.

This shows that the selected tutorial promotion effort helped improve tutorial visibility, increase engagement, and support product-related content exposure through more focused channel planning.
